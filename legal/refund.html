Refund & Cancellation

Effective: May 4, 2026 · Last updated: May 4, 2026

A clear, firm, and considerate policy. Our goal is to protect Catherine’s reserved time while making it easy for serious clients to move forward.

01Overview

A $25 booking fee is required to reserve your bridal consultation at The Fitting Room at Gray House. This fee holds your appointment time and compensates the studio for reserved availability.

If you proceed with alterations

Your $25 booking fee is credited toward your final alterations bill.

02Rescheduling

  • Each consultation may be rescheduled one time only.
  • Reschedule requests must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled appointment.
  • Reschedule requests submitted within 24 hours of the appointment forfeit the booking fee.
  • The new appointment must be scheduled within 60 days of the original date, subject to availability.

03When the booking fee is forfeited

The $25 booking fee is non-refundable in the following circumstances:

  • Cancellation by the client
  • Missed appointment (no-show)
  • Reschedule requests made within 24 hours of the appointment
  • A second reschedule on the same booking
  • Change of dress or change of mind
  • Scheduling conflicts identified by the client after booking

A new booking fee is required to schedule another consultation in any of the above circumstances.

04Exceptions for genuine emergencies

Catherine may, at her sole discretion, waive the forfeiture rule in cases of genuine emergency — serious illness, family emergency, severe weather, or comparable circumstances. Documentation may be requested.

The public-facing standard remains firm. Exceptions are not guaranteed and should not be assumed.

05Alterations work & deposits

Once a client moves forward with alterations, written estimates and timelines are provided before work begins. Deposits may be required for extensive work or custom commissions, and are detailed in the written work agreement.

Alteration work is custom by nature and is generally non-refundable once cutting, stitching, or fabric removal has begun. If a defect in our workmanship is identified, we will repair or remake the affected work at no additional cost.

06Refunds, processing fees, and chargebacks

In the rare cases where a refund is issued (for example, if Catherine cancels an appointment and cannot reschedule), the refund will be returned via the original payment method through Stripe. Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days.

Stripe’s payment processing fees, where applicable, may not be returned to the client and are excluded from any refund. Disputing a valid charge as a chargeback is not an appropriate substitute for the policy above; we reserve the right to provide all booking records to the issuing bank.

07Acknowledgement

By booking an appointment, you acknowledge and agree to this policy.

08Contact

Questions or reschedule requests should be directed to inquiry@thefittingroom-gh.com.

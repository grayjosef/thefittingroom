01Quick summary

  • We collect your name, email, phone, wedding date, and gown details so we can prepare for your appointment.
  • Payments are processed by Stripe; we never see or store your full card number.
  • We do not sell your data, ever.
  • You can request a copy or deletion at any time by emailing us.

02What we collect

Information you provide

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Wedding date and event details
  • Gown information (designer, shop, type of work needed, timeline)
  • Any notes you choose to include with your booking or inquiry
  • Policy agreement confirmation

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical data (device type, browser, screen size, IP region)
  • Pages visited on this site (for layout and accessibility improvements only)
  • Stripe collects payment card data under their PCI-DSS-compliant infrastructure; we receive only the transaction reference, the last four digits, and the amount.

03Why we collect it

  • Service delivery — to confirm appointments, prepare for your gown, and contact you with reminders or follow-ups.
  • Payment processing — to charge the booking fee and any subsequent alteration fees through Stripe.
  • Legal compliance — to keep records required by tax and small-business law in Wisconsin and the United States.
  • Site improvements — to understand which pages help clients book and which need work.

04Who we share with

We share information only with the small set of vendors required to run the Studio:

  • StripePayment processing. stripe.com/privacy
  • Google WorkspaceEmail, calendar, and consultation scheduling. policies.google.com/privacy
  • Hosting providerSite hosting and basic security telemetry.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. We will only disclose information when required by law or to protect the safety of our clients and staff.

05Cookies & analytics

This site uses a small number of strictly necessary cookies to operate the booking flow and remember your preferences. We use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to count page views; we do not use advertising cookies. Full details on our Cookie Notice page.

06How long we keep it

  • Booking records — up to 24 months after your wedding date.
  • Financial records — 7 years, as required by US tax law.
  • Email correspondence — 24 months from last contact.
  • Site analytics — 14 months in aggregate, anonymized form.

07Security

Our website is served over HTTPS. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe under PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance. Email and calendar systems are managed through Google Workspace with two-factor authentication enforced.

No system is perfectly secure, but we treat your data with the same care we treat your gown.

08Your rights

You may at any time:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications (we send very few)

How to exercise your rights

Email inquiry@thefittingroom-gh.com. We respond within 30 days.

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what categories of personal information we have collected and the right to opt out of any sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

09Children

Our services and website are intended for adults age 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information, please contact us so we can remove it.

10Changes

We may update this policy as our practices evolve. The effective date is shown at the top. Material changes will be highlighted on the homepage for at least 30 days.

Acceptable Use

Effective: May 4, 2026 · Last updated: May 4, 2026

A short, plain-English code for how the studio engages with clients, the website, and the payment system.

01Services we accept

The Studio currently accepts:

  • Bridal gowns and wedding-day attire
  • Mother of the bride and groom dresses
  • Bridesmaid dresses
  • Quinceañera, prom, and other formal-wear
  • Custom sewing inquiries (case by case, based on availability)

We do not accept everyday tailoring, dry-cleaning intake, costume work, or commercial production runs.

02Client conduct

The Studio is a private working space. Clients and any accompanying guests are expected to:

  • Treat Catherine and any other clients with courtesy.
  • Refrain from photography of other clients or work in progress without permission.
  • Refrain from bringing unannounced guests; the studio is small and time is reserved per appointment.
  • Refrain from intoxicants. Champagne for celebratory pickups may be welcomed at Catherine’s discretion.

The Studio reserves the right to end an appointment, decline future bookings, and forfeit booking fees in cases of harassment, abuse, theft, or destruction of property.

03Website use

You agree not to:

  • Submit fraudulent payment information or chargebacks for valid services.
  • Scrape, copy, or republish content from this site without written permission.
  • Attempt to disrupt, probe, or gain unauthorized access to the booking or payment system.
  • Use the contact form or email to send unsolicited commercial communication.

04Prohibited transactions

In line with our payment processor’s requirements, the booking system may not be used in connection with:

  • Any illegal activity under U.S. federal or Wisconsin state law.
  • Goods or services that infringe intellectual property rights.
  • Hate speech, harassment, or threats.
  • Any item or service prohibited under Stripe’s restricted businesses list.

05Enforcement

Violations may result in cancellation of bookings, forfeiture of fees, refusal of service, and where appropriate, referral to law enforcement. We reserve the right to update this policy at any time.

06Contact

Concerns about acceptable use should be sent to inquiry@thefittingroom-gh.com.

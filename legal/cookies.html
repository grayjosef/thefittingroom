Cookie Notice

Effective: May 4, 2026 · Last updated: May 4, 2026

We use a small, restrained set of cookies. No advertising, no third-party tracking. Just enough to make booking work and keep the site healthy.

01Strictly necessary

  • Session cookieMaintains your booking flow state while you complete a reservation. Expires when you close the tab.
  • Stripe cookiesSet by our payment processor to detect fraud and complete checkout. Stripe cookie policy
  • CSRF tokenProtects the booking form from cross-site request forgery.

02Privacy-respecting analytics

We measure aggregate page-view counts to understand which pages help clients book. The data is anonymized; no personal identifiers, fingerprints, or cross-site profiles are collected. No advertising network has access.

03Controlling cookies

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Blocking strictly necessary cookies will break the booking flow and payment confirmation, so a successful reservation requires that those remain enabled for the duration of checkout.
